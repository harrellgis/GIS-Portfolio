About Me

I work as a geospatial scientist, GIS developer, and cartographer to create geospatial solutions that address challenges facing global sustainable development in the 21st century.


My focus is on the interplay between global environmental change and human development, while I also strive to increase the availability of open source high-quality spatial data and cartographic products to communicate valuable spatial information to the public.


My hope is to make resources more available for conservation, development, and land management activities. Much of my focus is on creating and publishing spatial data unique to Kenya, where high-quality data is not yet accessible.
