About Me

Hi, my name is Christopher Harrell. I work as a geospatial analyst, consultant, and cartographer to create solutions that address challenges facing global sustainable development in the 21st century.


My focus is on the interplay between global environmental change and human development, while I also strive to increase the availability of open source high-quality spatial data and cartographic products to communicate valuable information to the public.


I created wapiGIS in order to make geospatial information more available for conservation, human development, and land management activities. Much of my focus is on analyzing and publishing spatial data unique to Kenya, where high-quality data is not yet readily accessible.
